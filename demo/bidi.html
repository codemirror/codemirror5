Demonstration of bi-directional text support. See the related blog post for more background.

You can set base direction of individual line (or multiple selected lines) to LTR/RTL/editor default by pressing Ctrl-Alt-Left/Right/Down (Mac) or Ctrl-Alt-,/./slash (other platforms) respectively. In this demo, Alt-L/R/E also work.
